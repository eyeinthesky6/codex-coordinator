# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Be respectful, constructive, and welcoming. Disagreement is fine; harassment, threats, discrimination, doxxing, and deliberate disruption are not.

Examples of positive participation include:

- giving specific, useful feedback;
- respecting different experience levels and viewpoints;
- accepting correction and taking responsibility for mistakes;
- protecting private project and task information.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove inappropriate contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when needed to protect the community.

Report conduct concerns privately to a maintainer through a private channel available on their GitHub profile. Do not place sensitive details in a public issue. For urgent abuse occurring on GitHub, use GitHub's Report Abuse feature.

Maintainers will handle reports as privately as reasonably possible and avoid conflicts of interest.
